World Wide Network

How can we inform and connect with the public about endangered species or topics that is both fun and interactive?

To inform is to tell a story through a cause and effect, how much does the effort of certain species contribute to our ecosystem. To connect is to engage with your audience with similar themes that they may already be familiar with and understand these crises from another perspective.
